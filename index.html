About the City

Kabul, the capital and largest city of Afghanistan, is a place rich in history, culture, and breathtaking landscapes. Nestled in a valley surrounded by rugged mountains, Kabul has been a center of trade, knowledge, and civilization for thousands of years. As one of the oldest cities in Central Asia, Kabul boasts a fascinating blend of tradition and modernity. With bustling bazaars, historic landmarks, and stunning natural beauty, Kabul offers visitors a unique experience filled with history, culture, and warm hospitality.

Top Attractions

Kabul is filled with historical and cultural treasures. Some must-visit places include:

Paghman Gardens

A picturesque retreat just outside the city, perfect for a peaceful getaway.

Kabul Zoo

A popular family destination featuring native and exotic wildlife.

Kah Faroshi Bird Market

A fascinating bazaar where birds of all kinds are bought and sold.

Lake Qargha

A beautiful reservoir ideal for picnics, boating, and relaxation.

Pul-e Khishti Bazaar

A lively market where you can explore traditional Afghan crafts, spices, and textiles.

Chehelseton Garden

Chehelseton Garden, a hidden gem in the heart of Kabul, invites visitors to experience the tranquility of its lush landscapes and rich history.
